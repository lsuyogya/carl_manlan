Carl Manlan is a global leader driving inclusive economic transformation, human sustainability, and social impact through community financing, strategic innovation, and mentorship.

Carl is committed to creating impactful initiatives that drive community transformation worldwide.

Carl’s impactful career spans leadership roles at influential organizations such as the Global Fund, UNDP, African Union, and Ecobank Foundation. He's widely recognized for his thought leadership in economic inclusion, health equity, and sustainable solutions. Carl is committed to nurturing the next generation through mentorship, sharing powerful ideas, and creating impactful initiatives that drive community transformation worldwide.
